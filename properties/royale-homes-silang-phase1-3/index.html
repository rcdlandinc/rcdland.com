Property Description

Live your suburban dreams in RCD Royale Homes Silang! With 1,066 housing units standing on 21 hectares of land, RCD Royale Homes Silang is a well-planned community that is complete with amenities such as a guarded entrance gate, a basketball court, a swimming pool, and a clubhouse. It is strategically located in a highly urbanized residential area which is accessible to schools, public markets, hospitals, churches and shopping malls. Getting to and from the Metro is hassle-free with public transportation that is easily accessible few minutes from E. Aguinaldo Highway, the subdivision is placed along East-West Road and a 4-km short drive from Cavite-Laguna Expressway (CALAX). The cold breeze can be felt with Tagaytay being 10 minutes away from the property. Set within a scenic and relaxing environment, this home is definitely your breath of fresh air from the city. For as low as ₱ 3.6 million, you can live the serene lifestyle you’ve always wanted here in RCD Royale Homes Silang!

How to Get There

Via Commute

  1. Ride a van to Robinson’s Dasmariñas.
  2. Take a jeep or bus to Silang/Tagaytay, ask to be dropped at Premiere Plaza.
  3. From Premiere Plaza, ride a tricycle to RCD Royale Homes Silang.

Via Private Vehicle

Via SLEX:

From Starmall Alabang, take SLEX, exit at Carmona, go straight. At SM Dasma, turn left toward Tagaytay via Aguinaldo Highway. Nearest landmark is Robinsons Premiere Plaza.

Via Daang Hari:

Drive along Daang Hari Road, pass District Imus Mall, then turn left toward Tagaytay via Aguinaldo Highway.
