How to Get There

VIA BUS

GUIDE: Ride the bus line labeled as San Agustin, Erjohn, Starliner, DLTB that can be found in Buendia in Pasay or PITX. Choose the route that is going to Silang/Tagaytay or. Get off at Premier Plaza Silang. From there, ride a tricycle and ask the driver to drop you off at RCD Royale Homes Silang Phase 4 - East-West Road Gate.

VIA PRIVATE VEHICLE

VIA SLEX Mamplasan Exit/CALAX

GUIDE: You can take the South Luzon Expressway (SLEX) and take the Mamplasan Exit and proceed to CALAX and take Aguinaldo Exit. Turn left to Aguinaldo Highway then turn right to East-West Road. Go straight until you reach RCD Royale Homes Silang Phase 4.

VIA CAVITEX Kawit Exit/Tanza-Trece Road

GUIDE: Use the road going to CAVITEX then take an exit going to Kawit, go straight in Antero Soriano Highway then turn left at Gen. Trias Drive. Turn Right at Prinza St. and continue to Crisanto M. De Los Reyes Ave. Then Turn left at East-West Road. Go straight until you reach RCD Royale Homes Silang Phase 4.

VIA CAVITEX Bacoor Exit/Aguinaldo Highway

GUIDE: Use the road going to CAVITEX then take an exit going to Bacoor, go straight in Aguinaldo Highway then turn right to East-West Road. Go straight until you reach RCD Royale Homes Silang Phase 4.
