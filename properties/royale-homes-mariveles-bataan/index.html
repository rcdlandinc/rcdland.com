Property Description

Sprawling over 10 hectares of land with plain terrain, RCD Royale Homes Bataan is a gated subdivision which offers 613 units of quality and affordable homes in a peaceful and tranquil community. Strategically located just along the threshold of Mariveles, the subdivision is just along the four-lane barangay road which also makes it easily accessible from the six-lane Roman Superhighway. A community that is well-planned, complete with amenities such as a guarded entrance gate, a basketball court, a swimming pool, and a clubhouse. Modern-designed townhouses and single-attached homes priced from ₱1.3 million are expected to rise in the area, specifically intended for the middle-segment earners.

How to Get There

Via Commute

  1. Bus: Bus stations of Bataan Transit and Genesis are located at Avenida, Cubao, and Pasay. You can ride a bus with the signage of “Mariveles” and be directly dropped at Brgy. Mt. View, Mariveles, Bataan. Our site is walkable from the highway but you can also call a friendly tricycle driver that will lead you straight to Royale Homes Bataan.

Via Private Vehicle

Via NLEX/SCTEX:

When driving from Metro Manila, you can take the North Luzon Expressway and take either the San Fernando toll exit or the Subic-Clark-Tarlac Expressway (SCTEX). Upon exiting San Fernando, proceed to Jose Abad Santos Ave, all the way to Lubao Pampanga and Dinalupihan Bataan. At the Layac junction, take the road leading to Roman Super Highway on the left all the way to Mariveles, Bataan. Travel time is 2-3 hours by car depending on the traffic.

Via Ferry:

The easiest way to get to Mariveles Bataan from Metro Manila is by travelling by ferry. This option reduces travel time from 4 hours to 1 hour. The Manila terminal is located in Esplanade Seaside Terminal in Mall of Asia and the port in Bataan is in Port Capinpin Orion. After arriving at Orion, ride a jeepney going to Mariveles and get dropped at Brgy. Mt. View, Mariveles, Bataan.
