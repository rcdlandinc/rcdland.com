How to Get There

VIA BUS

  • GUIDE: Ride the bus line labeled as San Agustin, Erjohn, BSC that can be found in Buendia in Pasay. Choose the route that is going to Balayan, Batangas. Get off at Waltermart Balayan and ride a jeep or tricycle going to Brgy. Sambat and ask the driver to be dropped off at RCD Royale Homes Balayan Site. From Metro Manila going to Tuy, Batangas, expect a 2-2.5 hours travel time.

PRIVATE VEHICLE

VIA SLEX Mamplasan Exit/CALAX

GUIDE: You can take the South Luzon Expressway (SLEX) and take the Mamplasan Exit and proceed to CALAX and take Santa Rosa-Tagaytay Exit. Go straight to Santa Rosa - Tagaytay Rd towards Tagaytay-Nasugbu Hwy. Turn Left at Palico-Balayan, Batangas Road then turn right at Balibago-Balayan Hwy. Go straight until you reach RCD Royale Homes Balayan, Batangas Site.

VIA CAVITEX Kawit Exit/Tanza-Trece Road

GUIDE: Use the road going to CAVITEX then take an exit going to Kawit, go straight in Antero Soriano Highway then turn left at Tanza-Trece Martires Road, go straight at Trece Martires-Indang Rd., Indang-Mendez Rd., and Mendez-Tagaytay Rd. until you reach Mayor’s drive then turn left. Go straight ahead and turn right at Tagaytay-Nasugbu Hwy. Turn Left at Palico-Balayan, Batangas Road then turn right at Balibago-Balayan Hwy. Go straight until you reach RCD Royale Homes Balayan, Batangas Site.

VIA CAVITEX Bacoor Exit/Aguinaldo Highway

GUIDE: Use the road going to CAVITEX then take an exit going to Bacoor, go straight in Aguinaldo Highway until you reach Tagaytay-Nasugbu Hwy. Turn Left at Palico-Balayan, Batangas Road then turn right at Balibago-Balayan Hwy. Go straight until you reach RCD Royale Homes Balayan, Batangas Site.
