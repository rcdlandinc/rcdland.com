RCD Land Connects with Future Homeowners at Pag-IBIG Housing Fair - South Luzon

RCD Land proudly participated in the Pag-IBIG Housing Fair South Luzon, held last April 27-28 at the Jose Rizal Coliseum in Calamba City, Laguna.

Author: Nyl   Category: South Luzon   Date: May 2026

In partnership with Pag-IBIG Fund, RCD Land continues its commitment to making affordable homeownership more accessible to Filipino families, staying true to its mission of improving lives through quality and attainable housing.

IN PHOTO: Pag-IBIG Fund Chief Executive Officer Marilyn C. Acosta visits the RCD Land booth during the first day of the Pag-IBIG Housing Fair.

The event served as a meaningful opportunity to connect with future homeowners from different sectors, allowing the team to personally present communities that go beyond affordability—offering families not just homes, but a future they can build together.

IN PHOTO: RCD Land Brokers Coordinator Sir Rjay Padilla together with Dinfinytm Realty agents and Mr. Marcell Coral, a homebuyer, during the first day of the Pag-IBIG Housing Fair – South Luzon

During the two-day event, RCD Land generated at least 620 inquiries and successfully recorded one reservation, reflecting strong interest and trust from attendees.

IN PHOTO: Ms. Aira Desuyo and Mr. Fernyl Rebibis of RCD Land Sales and Marketing, together with Pag-IBIG Housing Relationship Officer for RCD Land Ms. Sharmane Abela and RCD Land sellers during Day 2 of the Housing Fair.

Featured projects included RCD Royale Homes Tuy, RCD Royale Homes Balayan, RCD Royale Homes Silang, and RCD Villas de Trece—communities designed to provide comfortable living spaces within growing and accessible locations.

IN PHOTO: Balayan Site Officer Ms. Aira Desuyo receives a Certificate of Appreciation on behalf of RCD Land from Pag-IBIG officials during the Housing Fair’s closing program

Through events like this, RCD Land strengthens its partnership with Pag-IBIG Fund and reaffirms its dedication to building not just homes, but futures filled with hope, stability, and opportunity for every Filipino family.
